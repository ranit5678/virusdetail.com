What is a computer virus?

In computing terms, a virus is a software program that enters the computer system by attaching itself to another program and spread across the system by replicating itself.

Virus programs are written by expert computer programmers.They are created either to cause or just as a prank

Virus programmers are of two types:-

  • Hackers

    • Hackers are people who use their skills to enter the computer system without authorisation. They are computer entusiasts who play pranks with their knowledge.

  • Crackers

    • Crackers are malicious programmers who gain unauthorised access to computer system for purpose of stealing, altering or corrupting information.

How does a computer virus affect?

  • sharing files with other users,

  • visiting an infected website,

  • opening spam e-mail,or

  • downloadind free games, music, or programs from unknown sites.

What does a computer virus do?

Viruses can harm your computer by damaging programs,deleting files or reformatting the hard drive

What are the symptoms of a virus-infected computer?

  • Slow computer performance
  • Unexplained data loss
  • Frequent data loss
  • Unusual display on the screen
  • Computer restart on its own
