How can you protect your system from virus?

To protect your system against viruses, you have to follow these steps:

  • Install a good antivirus software in your computer

  • Use antimalware software.

  • Update your operating system and antivirus software regularly.

  • Use only trusted sites to to download software.

  • Alaways scan your CDs, pen drives and flash drives and flash drives before using them.

  • Do not use pirated software.

  • Turn on the firewall of your computer operating system.

  • Do not open messages from unknown senders.
